Privacy Policy

Whale Tracker App

Last updated: March 2026

Whale Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Whale Tracker mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the app:

  • Watchlist data — tickers you save are stored locally on your device only.
  • Alert preferences — notification settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage analytics — we do not collect any usage analytics or crash reports.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Data Sources

The app displays data from the following public sources:

  • SEC EDGAR — Public Form 4 insider trading filings (sec.gov)
  • Yahoo Finance — Real-time and historical stock price data
  • Reddit — Public posts from finance subreddits for sentiment analysis

All data displayed is publicly available. We do not scrape or store any private user data from these platforms.

3. How We Use Data

  • To display SEC insider trading filings from public records
  • To calculate Reddit sentiment scores from public posts
  • To show stock price information

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

4. Data Storage

Your watchlist and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard mobile storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The app communicates with our backend server hosted on Railway.app solely to fetch public financial data. No personal information is sent to this server.

Reddit data is accessed via the Reddit API in compliance with Reddit's Terms of Service and Developer Policy. We only access publicly available posts and do not store any Reddit user data.

6. Not Financial Advice

All content displayed in Whale Tracker — including insider trading data, sentiment scores, and market prices — is for informational purposes only. Nothing in this app constitutes financial, investment, or trading advice. Always do your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

7. Children's Privacy

Whale Tracker is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
